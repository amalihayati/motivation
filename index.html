Online education: Motivation Factor Towards Online Education

Here is a quote from publication website

Online learning or distance learning is an important part of University teaching around the word with the fact that there is a great need to provide flexible delivery of education. It is gaining popularity in the educational world nowadays. This trend will likely continue due to the globalization of education and the spread of new and adequate information technology tools. It gives flexibility to the learners in terms of time and place.

Here some of the motivation factor towards online education.

1. Learning in a modern way

I can learn in a more modern way and save time and learn lessons online. It connects students and faculty in furthering their learning even though we are all far away. In this way, we can conduct our learning sessions as usual and be able to adapt to this increasingly modern technology.

2. More effective education

Effective education in online learning can change the way students think it's not difficult. Effective online education can encourage students to stay interested in continuing to learn the knowledge delivered by the lecturers. Not only that, this online education has also enabled more students to become confident in their lessons.

3. Increase knowledge in technology

In this online learning can also enhance my knowledge in this increasingly modern field of technology. It can also make me a knowledgeable person. In fact, online education also shows that it is very important in today's learning. This can motivate me to continue learning online.
